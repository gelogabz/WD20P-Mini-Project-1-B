Ticketing Questions

GA PASS: Entry into the main festival grounds on Friday, Saturday and Sunday. Every one wishing to enter the festival will need their own pass.

VIP PASS: Entry into the main festival grounds on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday AND access to additional VIP areas. The main VIP Viewing Area is located adjacent to the Oceania Stage. VIPs enjoy more comfortable amenities including shade, premium restrooms, private bars and expedited entry into the festival.

GA Car Camping Passes: Vehicle entry into the GA campgrounds. Includes:
• One (1) 20x20 Car Camping Spot and can fit 1-2 tents and 1 standard size car, truck, or SUV
• There is no set limit on the amount of people allowed in Car Camping spots, but your vehicle and camping gear may not spill over into fire lanes.
• There is no “per person” camping fee. So if, for example, you have 3 people coming in one car, you only need 1 car camping pass (everyone will need their own festival ticket though).
All Campers must have a Festival Pass in addition to their Car camping pass to enter the campgrounds.

VIP Car Camping Passes: Premium Car camping close to the main festival entrance. This is per vehicle and includes:
• Premium car campsite close to the main festival entrance
• Includes one (1) 20x20 VIP Car Camping Spot and can comfortably fit 1-2 tents and 1 standard sized car, truck, or SUV.
• Includes 2 VIP Camping Wristbands (more “VIP Companion” wristbands can be purchased)
• Exclusive amenities to the VIP campground including comfort restrooms and shower trailers.

VIP Companion Camping Pass: Entry into the VIP campgrounds. This allows you to camp at Imagine as a companion to a VIP Car Camping Pass, allowing you to join up with your VIP friends and share a campsite. Please note that this “VIP Companion” Camping Pass does not include your own campsite or parking. Companion passes are available for the VIP campgrounds only.

Pool Party Tickets: Access to the pool parties from 12-3 on Saturday and Sunday, inside the festival. Tickets sold per day, if you would like to attend both days you will need to purchase both a Saturday and Sunday pass. Please print out your ticket in advance of the show and bring it with you, you can also pull it up on your mobile phone but sometimes phones die, so best to keep a hard copy. All pool party guests will also need to have a festival wristband to enter.

Thursday Pre-Party: This is a separately ticketed event. To attend, you will need to purchase an Early Arrival + Pre-Party pass, for which all tickets now come with early arrival. You have to be attending to the festival in order to attend this event.
